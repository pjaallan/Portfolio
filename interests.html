Interests

My interests away from IT include Rugby Union Football, having played the game as a young man, Glam Rock music from the UK in the 70's, the history of Tudor England and travelling to and touring the island of Tenerife in the Canary Islands.

The pictures here show the emblem of the England Rugby Football Union team worn on the player’s shirts, a portrait of the infamous Tudor King Henry the Eighth of England, who famously had six wives and beheaded two of them.

Slade, who are an iconic glam rock group in the UK and elsewhere, but are rather unknown in the US, although they are on record as having heavily influenced acts such as ‘Kiss’, ‘The Ramones’, Quiet Riot’, ‘Oasis’, ‘Nirvana’, and ‘Def Leopard’ amongst others.

The last photo is of Mount Teide, the 12,000 ft. volcano that stands at the centre of the island of Tenerife in the Canary Islands.
